# Game Zones - Pantheon

#### General Rules

1. The Pantheon is a private zone owned by a player. Each player has their own Pantheon zone
2. The Lesser and Greater Boons that a player selects and presents during a Pantheon match will start the game face down in this zone.
3. Players may play a Boon from this zone by bestowing the boon. This process is similar to that of activating or materializing a card, however, players can't have Opportunity until the boon has finished bestowing.
4. Players can look at their own face down boons at any time.
5. Boons in the Pantheon zone are not considered objects and can't be the target of activations, materializations, or abilities.
